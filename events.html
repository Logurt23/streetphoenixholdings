About Us

Let's throw the best motorcycle event ever, shall we?


Founded in 2019 by Daniel Burdick and Logan Brewer, Street Phoenix Holdings started developing assets in the tech industry. We created an environment that drives innovation, creativity, and inventiveness. The combination of Daniel's love for motorcycles and Logan's tech expertise, this event was born.

Our mission is simply one thing; to create the best motorcycle event in the country. We back the blue and will donate the proceeds from the activities of the event to Thin Blue Line USA to support our officers.

You've been specifically picked as someone we'd like to have at this event. Your ticket purchase means you'll be apart of a group of Americans that not only love cycling like you, but support our officers, military, and first responders.

Our event is one of a kind and exclusive. We hope you have a wonderful time and enjoy the scenery that the Dragons Tail has to offer!
